Why it matters

What we're driving toward

A structured, scalable approach to create better ways of working.

Objective 01

Standardize and scale processes

A transparent scoring model and reusable delivery methods to standardize, optimize, and scale how work happens across the department.

Objective 02

Focus effort on the highest-value problems

Projects are prioritized by value and ease, so we take on the work with the highest return on efficiency, quality, and cost.

Objective 03

Absorb complexity, remove barriers

As the portfolio grows in breadth and complexity, build ways of working that absorb it — and strip out the operational barriers that slow delivery down.

Objective 04

Make improvements that last and take hold

Build solutions that hold after the project closes — documented, owned, and measured — and grow OpEx practices so they become part of how the team operates.
