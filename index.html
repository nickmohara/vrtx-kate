01 — Charter

What this function is for

We make the way the department delivers work more consistent, efficient, and measurable — standardizing how projects are taken in, prioritized, and run, and driving continuous improvement across every domain.

Engineering Capital Projects Facilities Lab Oversight Real Estate Global Security
Standardize, then improve
Establish a repeatable baseline before optimizing. You can't improve a process that isn't defined.
One front door
All work enters through a single intake path, so nothing is invisible and everything is prioritized on the same basis.
Decisions from data
Prioritization and performance claims are backed by evidence — not the volume or seniority of who's asking.
Process before tools
Fix the workflow first. Technology amplifies a good process and entrenches a bad one.
02 — Goals & Objectives

What we're driving toward

Outcomes, not activities — so the team can see the direction and measure progress against it.

Objective 01

A single, trusted intake path

Every request enters one front door with a predictable response, replacing scattered emails and hallway asks.

Objective 02

Fair, visible prioritization

A transparent scoring model that ranks work by value and risk — so the portfolio reflects what matters most.

Objective 03

Standardized delivery

Core processes documented and reused across domains, cutting cycle time and coordination overhead.

Objective 04

Reporting leaders rely on

Live portfolio and performance views that make decisions faster and easier to defend.

05 — Continuous Improvement

How we get better over time

A deliberate loop — not a heavy certification program — that turns recurring problems into standard fixes.
