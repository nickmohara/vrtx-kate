02 — Governance

Who decides, and how

How decisions get made, who holds which authority, where work is reviewed, and how to escalate when something is stuck — so accountability is clear and every decision has an owner.

Decision rights

RoleResponsibility
Executive SponsorOperations & RE VPEnsures the OpEx function stays aligned to functional goals, owns final sign-off on portfolio prioritization and strategy & framework changes, and serves as the final escalation point when decisions exceed the project sponsor's authority.
Operations & RE LTGoverns the portfolio collectively — aligning on priorities, resourcing, and strategy at the monthly portfolio review and quarterly strategy & framework review.
Project SponsorOperations & RE LT memberAccountable for strategic alignment, key decisions, removing roadblocks, and ensuring the project delivers its intended business value.
Project TeamExecutes the project deliverables in accordance with the approved charter — the individuals doing the work, tracking progress and managing blockers.
OpEx TeamOwns the day-to-day operation of the OpEx function, maintains and continuously improves the OpEx framework, manages the portfolio governance process, facilitates review forums, and guides projects through the operating model from intake to close.

Governance forums

ForumCadenceDecides
Intake & Triage ReviewWeeklyAccept / decline, score, and prioritize new requests; unblock active work. Exceptions needing Operations & RE LT input are escalated from this forum.
Portfolio ReviewMonthlyStatus updates, reprioritization, resourcing, and stage-gate go / no-go decisions. Includes benefit validation and closure / handoff review with Operations & RE LT.
Strategy & Framework ReviewQuarterlyGoals, scoring weights, SOPs, and the roadmap of what to build next. Operations & RE LT weigh in on any proposed changes.

All forums are led by the OpEx team.

Escalation path

01
Raise
Project team flags the blocker on the project, with impact and the decision needed.
02
Project lead
Resolves within 2 business days where it's theirs to call.
03
OpEx Team
Assesses and resolves at the weekly triage review if the project lead is not able to solution.
04
Project / Executive Sponsor
Only true exceptions — money, risk, or conflicting priorities.

Change control

Any in-flight request must submit a change request if it impacts timeline, budget, scope, resource allocation, and/or compliance obligation. The OpEx team will assess the proposed change and consult the appropriate governing bodies before a final decision is made.
