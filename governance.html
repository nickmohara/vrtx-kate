Escalation path

01
Raise
Project team flags the blocker on the project, with impact and the decision needed.
02
Project lead
Resolves within 2 business days where it's theirs to call.
03
OpEx Lead
Assesses and resolves at the weekly triage review if the project lead is not able to solution.
04
Operations & RE LT
Only true exceptions — money, risk, or conflicting priorities.

Change control

Any in-flight request must submit a change request if it impacts timeline, budget, scope, resource allocation, and/or compliance obligation. The OpEx Lead + Owner will assess the proposed change before moving forward, and consult Operations & RE LT if needed.
