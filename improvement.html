04 — Continuous Improvement

How we get better over time

A deliberate loop — not a heavy certification program — that turns recurring problems into standard fixes.

P
Plan
Define the problem, measure the baseline, find root cause.
D
Do
Pilot a change on a small scale.
C
Check
Measure the result against the baseline.
A
Act
Standardize what worked; restart on what didn't.
Where ideas come from

An improvement backlog

Sourced from closeout lessons, recurring intake themes, metric outliers, and direct team input — groomed each quarter.

How we work a problem

A single-page A3

Background, current state, root cause, target, countermeasures, and follow-up — kept rigorous and shareable.
