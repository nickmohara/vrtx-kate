◆ Our Focus

The pillars our work traces to

Every project on the portfolio advances one of these. If it doesn't fit a pillar, it doesn't enter the portfolio.

Pillar 01Compliance & Risk
Projects driven by regulatory, safety, code, lease/legal, or audit requirements across facilities, real estate, and operations.
Pillar 02Growth & Scalability
New sites, expansions, and capital projects tied to footprint or capacity growth, and lease strategy for new space.
Pillar 03Operational Efficiency
Process improvement, cost reduction, and/or standardization within existing operations.
Pillar 04Facilities & Asset Performance
Maintenance programs, building systems, space utilization, and vendor management for existing physical assets.
Pillar 05Workplace Experience & Culture
Space planning for employee experience, safety culture, and how the environment supports the way people work.

These pillars are tied directly to the Priority Matrix within the Operating Model.
